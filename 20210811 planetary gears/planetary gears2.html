ring gear teeth:
R = A053144(n) = A002110(n) - A005867(n).

planet gear teeth:
P = A337022(n) = A002110(n)/2 - A005867(n).

sun gear teeth:
S = A005867(n).

For example for n=4:

R = A053144(4)=A002110(4) - A005867(4)=210-48=162
P = A337022(4)=A002110(4)/2 - A005867(4)=57
S = A005867(4)=48

To create this planetary gear for n=4, the openSCAD from this Thingiverse gear bearing was used.
