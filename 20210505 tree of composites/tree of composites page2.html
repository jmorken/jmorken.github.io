Proportions:

For the range 1 to 30 for n:

(sort the below by the first occurrences and not in a binary pattern)

000 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers don't have any of prime factors 2 or 3 or 5.
001 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers have prime factor 2 and not 3.
010 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers have prime factor 3 and not 2.
011 occurs 1/ of the time.  So 1/ of numbers have both prime factors 2 and 3.
100 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers don't have either prime factor 2 or 3.
101 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers have prime factor 2 and not 3.
110 occurs 1/ of the time, so 1/ of numbers have prime factor 3 and not 2.
111 occurs 1/30 of the time.  So 1/ of numbers have all prime factors 2 and 3 and 5.

These proportions sum to 1, corresponding to all natural numbers.

1/+1/+1/+1/+1/+1/+1/+1/=1.
